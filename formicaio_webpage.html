How to use Formicaio

Formicaio is an interactive chat experience about work and automation with an AI agent. Note: This is a desktop experience. Please use a computer and Google Chrome for the best interaction.

CONTROLS
  • • Click/press Enter to advance
  • • Type in chat when prompted
  • • Sound on recommended
  • • Takes 10-15 minutes
CONVERSATION EXAMPLES
  • "Lately I've been questioning if my creative work still matters in this AI era"
  • "The office culture kills me, but my team feels like family"
  • "I dream of a future where humans and machines truly collaborate"
  • "Sometimes I wonder if AI understands loneliness like we do"
  • "The line between work and life keeps blurring since the pandemic"
  • "Maybe automation will free us to pursue what really matters"
TIPS
  • • These are suggested interactions but you can virtually chat about every topic!
  • • Try to be as honest as possible, there are no right or wrong answers
  • • We don't collect your answers, so your feedback is important!
  • • Take your time
